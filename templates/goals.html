Aim
Sugarcane crops hold immense significance in India, being a crucial crop cultivated by numerous farmers for profitable returns. Protecting these crops from diseases like red rot and red rust is of paramount importance in order to enhance crop yield. To achieve this, we employ image classification techniques to distinguish between healthy and unhealthy batches of sugarcane, enabling us to swiftly identify and remove diseased plants from the fields before they inflict further harm upon the rest of the crop. By promptly detecting and eliminating the affected plants, we can safeguard the overall health and productivity of the sugarcane crop, ensuring optimal yields for the farmers.
